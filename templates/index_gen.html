How do payments work?

We hold funds in escrow when a project starts, ensuring freelancers get paid and clients receive their deliverables. Payments are released automatically upon completion or manually after client approval.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

Our dispute resolution team mediates any issues fairly. We offer revision periods, partial refunds, and project reassignments when necessary to ensure both parties are satisfied.

How long does it take to get approved?

Most freelancer profiles are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Clients can start posting projects immediately after account verification, which typically takes just a few minutes.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees ever. Freelancers pay 10% on earnings, clients pay 10% on project totals. Payment processing and platform features are included at no extra cost.
